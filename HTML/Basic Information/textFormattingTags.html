RAM NAVAMI


Introduction


Ram Navami is a major Hindu festival celebrating the birth of Lord Rama, the seventh avatar of Vishnu, marking the victory of dharma over adharma. Occurring on the ninth day (Navami) of Shukla Paksha in the Chaitra month (March-April), it concludes the nine-day Chaitra Navratri festivities. It features fasting, prayer, bhajan singing, and recitations of the Ramayana
